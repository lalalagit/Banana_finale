Blinchicks project

Pancakes, wherever you are!

The Problem

Nowdays a lot of old people stay alone at home and don't have the ability to earn money or meet new people. They'd love to change that. On the other hand there are students, who don't have much money and they're always hungry.

Target audience

Grandmas and elderly people, who need extra money and attention

Students, who'd love to eat pancakes and have mobile phones

Idea

What if we let grandmas make pancakes and feed them to young guys nearby? The heve a lot of time to make pancakes and can earn.

Students download the app and see pancakes located nearby, so they can call the granny and get some food

How we earn

For students the app will cost 2$ to see the place nearby, where pancakes can be ready in 2 hours. They pick a place and app sends request to the chosen Grandma

Grandmas get 1$ for each student pancaked

Security

We love Grandmas, so students have to first verify their identity
